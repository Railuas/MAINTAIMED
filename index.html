Our Policies

Cancellation and No-Show Policy

Every client is important to us, and we understand that things come up. To give your appointment the attention it deserves, we request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or changes. Without 24-hour notice, a 50% charge of your booked service will apply.

Late Policy

Please be sure to arrive on time for your appointment. Arriving late may result in your appointment being rescheduled or canceled. Please call us on (869-664-4516) if you're going to be over 10 minutes late.

Refund Policy

  • Services received cannot be refunded. We will gladly adjust any service within two days of your appointment. Please return to the salon for a visual inspection, and we will work to correct any issues to your satisfaction.
  • A non-refundable deposit of $50 is required when booking your appointment. This deposit will be applied to your final bill. Cancellations or no-shows result in 100% deposit forfeit.

Hair Prep

Services include shampoo, conditioner, and detangling. Please ensure all braids, twists, and previous styles are removed before your appointment. If your service doesn't include a wash, make sure your hair is product-free.

Extra Guests

NO extra guests (unless you are a parent of a child) are allowed during the time of your service. They will be asked to leave to ensure we are able to give you the attention you deserve during your service.
